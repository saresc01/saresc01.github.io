WELCOME TO

ESBJERG SHOTING CLUB

We are one of the shoting club in Esbjerg. You have the oportunity to try this sport in the introduction to new shooters that takes place on Thursday at 17:30 in odd-numbered weeks. Here you will hear the association, safety on the ranges and general shooting technique. After the introduction, there will be an opportunity to try shooting with a rifle and/or pistol. The first 3 times membership is not necessary and therefore this only costs ammo.
